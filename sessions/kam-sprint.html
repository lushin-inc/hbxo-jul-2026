Why a sprint instead of the full series

  • This team is already winning. They don't need nine weeks of fundamentals — they need four sessions on the one thing holding them back.
  • That thing is reach. Every rep owns a product and defends it. Nobody's confidently opening a new conversation inside an account they already own.
  • Craig's read: get the meeting first, learn the product second. Behavior over expertise.
  • Referrals only work for one rep today — Jon Kane is the only one with a real network. Everyone else is cold.
  • Accounts like ASSA are multi-division and only one division knows us. That's the upside the sprint is aimed at.
